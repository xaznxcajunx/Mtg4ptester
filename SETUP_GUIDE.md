## Quick Start Guide
1. Open the web application in your browser.
2. Click on the install icon in the address bar (if available).
3. Follow the prompts to install the PWA on your device.

## Installation Instructions

### Android
1. Open Chrome on your Android device.
2. Navigate to the web app’s URL.
3. Tap the menu icon (three dots in the corner).
4. Select "Add to Home screen."
5. Confirm the installation by tapping "Add."

### iOS
1. Open Safari on your iOS device.
2. Navigate to the web app’s URL.
3. Tap the Share icon (square with an arrow).
4. Select "Add to Home Screen."
5. Confirm by tapping "Add."

### Windows
1. Open Microsoft Edge on your Windows PC.
2. Navigate to the web app’s URL.
3. Click the menu icon (three dots).
4. Select "Apps" and then "Install this site as an app."
5. Confirm the installation.

### Mac
1. Open Safari on your Mac.
2. Navigate to the web app’s URL.
3. Click on the Share icon in the menu bar.
4. Select "Add to Home Screen."
5. Confirm by clicking "Add."

### Linux
1. Open a Chromium-based browser (like Chrome or Chromium).
2. Navigate to the web app’s URL.
3. Click the menu icon (three dots).
4. Select "Install" or "Add to Desktop."
5. Follow the prompts to finish the installation.

## Troubleshooting
- If the app does not install, ensure that you are using a supported browser.
- Clear your browser cache and try again.
- Ensure that you are connected to the internet.

## Conclusion
Follow the above instructions for installing the PWA on your respective platform. For any further assistance, please consult the troubleshooting section or reach out for support.
